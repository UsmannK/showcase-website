The Purdue Computing Student Council presents

Computing Showcase

When is this?

Career Fair: Jan. 29th from 9am to 3pm
Hackathon: Jan. 29th and 30th (Starting at 6pm)


What is this?

Purdue Computing Showcase is a unique event where employers and students will have opportunities to engage and demonstrate skills beyond a resume. The way this manifests itself is through a more professional focused event (Career Fair) followed by a Hackathon where students can spend more time with recruiters informally.
